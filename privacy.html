Privacy & Compliance Statement

Last updated: 2025-05-26

No Personal Data Collection

Culture Flip is a static educational website that does not ask users to create accounts, enter names, e‑mail addresses, or any other personal identifiers. All interactions occur entirely within the user’s device.

Technical Logs

Our hosting platform (GitHub Pages) may retain minimal, non‑identifying technical logs (IP address, browser type) for up to 7 days solely for security and operational purposes. These logs are automatically deleted and are never combined to create user profiles.

Local Storage

We may use your browser’s localStorage to remember which cards you have already flipped. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time via your browser settings.

COPPA & GDPR

  • COPPA: Because we do not collect, store, or transmit personal information from children under 13, parental consent is not required.
  • GDPR & CCPA: The site processes no personal data; therefore, data‑subject rights requests are not applicable.
